Challenge Description

Mary Had a Little Something

Create a form that collects Name, Adjective, and Noun from the user. When the form is submitted, it updates the Mary Had a Little Lamb poem to reflect the user supplied Name, Adjective, and Noun (e.g. Tommy Had a Large Dog).

  • Using CSS, The populated results should distinguish themselves from the surrounding unpopulated text (e.g. add background colors)
  • Short Story must be approximately 2-3 paragraphs, can be found or made up.
  • Update the Title tags, and the H1 as a phrase
